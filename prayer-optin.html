Cedar Grove Prayer Text Messages

Cedar Grove Christian Church offers a text message program to share prayer requests and occasional ministry updates with our congregation.

How to Subscribe

To receive prayer text messages, text PRAYER to (423) 820-2920.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 10 messages per day depending on prayer needs and church updates.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.
For assistance, reply HELP.
