--- layout: default title: Privacy Policy | Cedar Grove Christian Church description: --- _Last updated: February 5, 2026_ ## Overview Cedar Grove Christian Church (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and when it may be shared. ## Information We Collect Depending on how you interact with our website and services, we may collect: - **Contact information you provide** (such as name, email address, phone number) - **Form submissions** (such as visitor information, prayer requests, volunteer sign-ups, event registrations) - **Message content you provide** (for example, if you text a request to our church phone number) - **Basic technical data** (such as IP address, device/browser type, pages visited, and approximate location derived from IP) collected through normal website logs and security tools ## How We Use Information We use the information we collect to: - Respond to requests and communicate with you - Provide church-related services (visitor follow-up, prayer request handling, event coordination, volunteer coordination) - Operate, maintain, and secure our website and systems - Improve website reliability and user experience ## Text Messaging (SMS) and Prayer Requests If you text our church number, we may store your phone number and the content of your message to process your request (for example, for review/approval workflows and distribution to subscribers when applicable). We do **not** use SMS messages for marketing. ## Sharing of Information We **do not sell** your personal information and we **do not share it for marketing or advertising purposes**. We may share information only in these limited cases: - **Service providers** that help us operate our services (for example, SMS delivery, website hosting, security, or email services). These providers may process information only to perform services for us. - **Legal requirements** if we are required to comply with law, regulation, subpoena, or court order. - **Safety/security** to protect the rights, safety, and security of our church, our users, or others. ## Data Retention We keep information only as long as needed for church operations and recordkeeping, or as required by law. We may delete information when it is no longer needed. ## Your Choices You may contact us to: - Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information (when applicable) - Ask questions about how your information is used - Opt out of non-essential communications If you are subscribed to SMS updates and want to stop receiving texts, reply **STOP** (where supported) or contact us. ## Security We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No online system can be guaranteed 100% secure. ## Children’s Privacy Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without appropriate consent. ## Contact Us Cedar Grove Christian Church 5270 Chuckey Pike Chuckey, TN 37641 contact@cedargrovechristian.com
