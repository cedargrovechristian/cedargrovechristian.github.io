Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

Overview

Cedar Grove Christian Church (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and when it may be shared.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website or services:

  • Information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, or phone number
  • Form submissions, including visitor information, prayer requests, volunteer sign-ups, and event registrations
  • Message content you send to us, including SMS text messages
  • Basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited

How We Use Information

Information collected is used only for church-related purposes, including:

  • Responding to inquiries and requests
  • Processing visitor and prayer request submissions
  • Coordinating church events, volunteers, and communications
  • Operating, maintaining, and securing our website and systems

Text Messaging (SMS)

If you send a text message to our church phone number, we may collect and store your phone number and message content in order to process and respond to your request.

SMS messages are not used for marketing or advertising purposes.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information and we do not share personal information for marketing or advertising purposes.

Information may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers that help operate our services (such as SMS delivery, website hosting, or security services)
  • When required to comply with applicable laws or legal processes
  • To protect the safety, security, or rights of the church or others

Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary for church operations, recordkeeping, or legal requirements, and may be deleted when no longer needed.

Your Choices

You may contact us to:

  • Request access to or correction of your information
  • Request deletion of your information when applicable
  • Ask questions about how your information is used

If you are subscribed to SMS updates and wish to stop receiving messages, you may reply STOP where supported or contact us directly.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without consent.
