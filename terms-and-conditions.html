Cedar Grove Christian Church provides an SMS program to share church-related information such as announcements, schedule updates, event information, and (if you opt in) prayer request updates. Messages may include content submitted by members/attendees (for example, prayer requests) and may be reviewed before distribution.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on church activity and updates.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier may charge fees for sending or receiving text messages, especially if you do not have an unlimited texting or data plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send STOP, you may receive one final confirmation message, and then you will no longer receive SMS messages from us unless you opt in again.
